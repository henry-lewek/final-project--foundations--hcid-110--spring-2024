Design Communication at MST

During my junior and senior year of high school, aside from attending my normal school, I joined a program called Design Communication or DesCom at the Manchester School of Technology. The program was focused on learning the design process as well as architecture during the second year. Ranging from drafting to using AutoCAD, the entire 2 years gave me valuable skills needed if I want to pursue a career in any of those fields.
